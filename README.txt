Dish Warrior
Created by Kevin Hoffman

Dish Warrior is a game created with JavaScript and HTML canvas.  Evil dishes have invaded and it is your mission to take care of them!  There are three "bosses" to destroy and the game gets increasingly difficult as time progresses.  Every time a dish gets past you, you lose 10 points of health.  You can also gain health by destroying pan dishes and also bosses.  Good luck!
